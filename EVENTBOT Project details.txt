Project Title: EVENT BOT
Project description:The company on an institution organising the event can setup the CHATBOT to answer the common questions like:
What are the details of the event?
What are the different domains in the event?
What is the registration process for particular domain?
CHATBOT takes name and email id of the user and gets registered to the event.
It also provides the modes of the payment to get registered.
